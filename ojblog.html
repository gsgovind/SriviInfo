Earn money through blog

Blog is used to share your ideas and expertise with the people around the world. Coding knowledge like HTML, CSS not required to create a blog. There are many templates are available online, you just need to update the content. Follow the below steps for a successful blog.

  • When you decided to start a blog, choose an unique topic first.
  • Design your blog with the content using any of the tools like WordPress etc.
  • Choose a name and hosting (Free or Paid) provider then update the contents
  • Optimize the content for Search Engines. Ensure that the page title should not exceed 70 characters. Google reads only 70 characters from the title.
  • Regularly add a new content to attract more visitors.
  • Share your links with others to get more traffic.
