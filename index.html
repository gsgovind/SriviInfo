Srivilliputhur - A Divine City

Srivilliputhur is a town (First Grade Municipality) in Virudhunagar District in TamilNadu State located at south west of Madurai by a distance of 74 KM. You can view the Srivilliputtur Map in "Srivilliputhur Map" page.

Srivilliputhur is one of the 108 divya desams of Lord Sri Vishnu. The main attraction in Srivilliputhur apart from Sri Andal Temple is 11 tiered tower constructed for Lord Vatapatrasayee. The 192 feet high tower is the Official Emblem of Government of Tamilnadu. Lots of other holy places can be visited in and around Srivilliputhur.

Srivilliputhur also has water falls, forests & a Giant squirrel sanctuary and you can get more information in "Places to visit" page. Srivilliputhur can be visited any time / seasons of the year. Srivilliputhur is well connected by Roads and also has Train Services from Chennai(Madras) and Madurai. The travel details are given in the "Reach Srivilliputhur" page.

Refer the "Hotels and accommodations" page for a comfortable stay. To know more about the festivals in Srivilliputhur, refer "Festivals" page. The general information like pincode, bank IFSC code are given in the "GeneralInfo" page.

Srivilliputhur is also very famous for a delicious milk sweet which is called "PalKova" in regional language. Srivilliputhur Palkova is as famous as Tirunelveli Halwa. The Srivilliputhur Milk Producers Cooperative Society Ltd and other local vendors are preparing Palkova. Srivilliputhur Palkova is available in most of the leading grocery stores and online to can be purchased online.

Handloom weaving and Agri works are the primary work in Srivilliputhur. Another uniqueness in Srivilliputhur is that the District Court is in Srivilliputhur which is not a district head quarters.
