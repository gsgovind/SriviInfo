How to reach Srivilliputhur?

Srivilliputhur can be reached by Road / Train.

By Air

Madurai is the nearest Airport from Srivilliputhur. Srivilliputhur can be reached by bus / car / train from Madurai.

Flights from Chennai (Also called as Madras) to Madurai

By Train

Srivilliputhur has a railway station and can be reached from Chennai via "Pothigai Express" and Madurai is the nearest railway station where frequent trains are available from other cities. Regular Passenger trains are running from Madurai to Srivilliputhur. Refer the beow link to know the timing of trains from Madurai to Srivilliputhur.

Trains scheduled from Madurai to Srivilliputhur

Trains scheduled from Srivilliputhur to Madurai

Srivilliputhur can be reached by bus / car from Madurai Railway station if you do not like passenger trains. Private taxi services available from Madurai Railway station to Srivilliputh ur. When you plan to travel by Corporation bus then need to go to Mattuthavani Bus stand in Madurai. Buses are available to Srivilliputhur every 10 Mins.

By Road

Srivilliputhur can be reached by cars & buses From Madurai. When you come by Corporation bus, need to take the bus at Mattuthavani Bus stand in Madurai. Buses are available to Srivilliputhur every 10 Mins and running in night also, so no need to worry about the bus service.
